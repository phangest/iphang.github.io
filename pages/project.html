"I REGISTER MY OPINION"

2017, University of Benin, Ekehuan Campus

I register my opinion project creates a platform for the public to express their thoughts, ideas and identities through colors, with the use of the palms to stamp colors of thier choice on a large canvas surface collaboratively. The project was staged to create a unified platform where different identities could coexist and interact through colors.

"A VISUAL REPRESENTATION ON THE INFLUENCE OF SOCIAL MEDIA ON SUICIDAL TENDENCIES"

2019, Department of Fine and Applied Arts
University of Benin

The project explores the visual representation of the psycological influences of social media on suicidal thoughts and behaviours. Executed using acrylic color on a 7ft x 5ft canvas.
