Iphang-ishor David Ngaji was born in Nigeria, where he earned his B.A degree from the University of Benin (First Class Honours) in 2019. He draws influences from Human interactions with faith, psychology, nature and History, which he expresses by experimenting through painting, with various mediums and surfaces.
A firm believer in the words of the notable Russian writer, Fyodor Dostoyevsky "Beauty will save the world", He describes his practice of painting as a journey into the universal existence and experiences of life to reveal beauty.
He has participated in various art exhibitions and competitions in Nigeria, a finalist in 'Life in my city' art festival 2018 and served as a volunteer at the occupational therapy department at the University of Benin Teaching Hospital (UBTH), and has been published in the 2017 Ake Review Magazine as a contributor.
